DIEGO ZAMANA

Graphic Designer at Centro Universitário Belas Artes in São Paulo, and specialized in Editorial Design at Senac São Paulo.

After more than 10 years working in design, marketing and marketing agencies, I migrated to web design with a focus on Design Thinking and UI, developing web management systems and monitoring applications for multinationals such as Rentokil, Bob's, Manserv, VisualCue and Klabin.
